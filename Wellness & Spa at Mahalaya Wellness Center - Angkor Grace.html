Mahalaya Wellness Center

Ancient Angkorians practiced wellness in dedicated facilities in the temple complex. That wisdom continues at Mahalaya Wellness Center in the form of traditional Khmer massage and hot and cold ritual bathing pools. Integrating other traditional practices of Aruyvedic oil massage and Chinese Medicine, our therapists mindfully encourage your body, mind and spirit to rejuvenate.
